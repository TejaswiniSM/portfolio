About Me

Devops Engineer!

Me, Tejaswini SM I am a Electronics and Communication Engineering graduate from MVJ College of Engineering in Bangalore, with a strong academic background highlighted by a CGPA of 8.5. I has a diverse set of experiences, primarily in the field of VLSI design and data analysis, along with a variety of projects and certifications.
During an internship at Banashree Renewable Energy Pvt Ltd, I have gained proficiency in physical design and fabrication, focusing on layout design using tools like Cadence Virtuoso. Additionally, as a Data Analyst Intern at KPMG Forage, I engaged in practical tasks involving data quality assessment, data insights, and presentation.My project work includes diverse undertakings, such as Infrasonic Sound Detection using STM-based Microcontroller, an Amazon website project, a portfolio, and live projects involving the uploading of a web project to AWS Amplify and AWS EC2 instances using GitBash. I holds certificates in Python and Advanced SQL, and I have published conference papers on Infrasonic Sound Detection using STM-based microcontrollers and Designing cost-effective smart home lighting solutions.In terms of skills, I have a knowledge in HTML, CSS, Python, DevOps, Linux OS, Cloud Computing, AWS services, GIT, Jenkins, Ansible, Docker, Kubernetes, and SQL.
My strengths lie in a sharp eye for detail, a combination of hard and smart work, and strategic leadership, with a long-term vision and effective decision-making capabilities.
I have been achieved recognition through awards in the National Painting competition and a Python Coding Contest, showcasing a multifaceted skill set and a strong academic as well as practical foundation in engineering, data analysis, and technology.
